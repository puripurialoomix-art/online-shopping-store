Product Details

Skechers Slip-ins: Glide-Step Altus - Aphtur™ – Advanced Running Performance

Reach new speeds with enhanced comfort and cutting-edge performance technology in the Skechers GO RUN Razor 5™. This race-day ready running shoe is engineered for speed, stability, and responsiveness. Featuring an ultra-light HYPER BURST PRO™ midsole, a carbon-infused plate, and a precision-tuned Arch Fit® insole system, it’s ideal for runners looking to maximize performance on the track or road.

Product Highlights

  • Carbon-Infused Plate for optimal energy return
  • HYPER ARC™ rocker bottom for smoother transitions
  • Machine washable construction for easy care
  • 100% Vegan materials used in construction

Comfort & Performance Technologies

Arch Fit® Insole System

The patented Arch Fit® system is podiatrist-designed, developed with over 20 years of data and 120,000 unweighted foot scans. This removable insole provides arch support that molds to your foot, reducing impact and evenly distributing weight for improved comfort and reduced fatigue.

HYPER BURST PRO™ Midsole

Crafted using an advanced supercritical foaming process, HYPER BURST PRO™ delivers highly responsive and ultra-lightweight cushioning. This TPU-based technology ensures long-lasting resilience and energy return for serious runners.

HYPER ARC™ Technology

The HYPER ARC™ geometry is designed to promote a more efficient stride, improving running mechanics by allowing a smoother heel-to-toe transition. Ideal for both training and race-day runs.

Carbon-Infused Forefoot Plate

The patent-pending carbon plate enhances stability and propels the foot forward with every step. It offers an exceptional stiffness-to-weight ratio, giving runners the edge needed for peak performance.

Design & Construction Details

  • Lightweight engineered mesh upper offers breathability and a secure fit
  • Performance-driven lace-up running silhouette designed for speed and daily mileage
  • Goodyear® Rubber Outsole for reliable traction, durability, and road grip
  • 1.5-inch heel height
  • Stack Height: 30mm (heel) / 26mm (forefoot)
  • Heel-to-Toe Offset: 4mm
  • Weight: Approx. 8 oz / 227g (Men’s US Size 9)
  • Machine washable for added convenience
  • Vegan-friendly materials used throughout
  • Brand detailing with Skechers GO RUN® and Arch Fit® logos
