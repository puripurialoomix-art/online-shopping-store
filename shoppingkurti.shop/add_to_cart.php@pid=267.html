Product Details

Skechers Hands Free Slip-ins®: BOBS® Sun Ray - Bright Take

Go Hands Free™ and enjoy a casual blend of comfort and style with the Skechers Hands Free Slip-ins®: BOBS® from Skechers® Sun Ray - Bright Take. This laid-back sandal is designed for effortless wear and maximum comfort, making it perfect for warm-weather outings, vacations, or daily errands. With its eco-conscious construction and soft linen upper, this sandal delivers a relaxed look and a supportive, comfortable feel.

Product Highlights

  • Hands Free Slip-ins® design with Heel Pillow™
  • Skechers Plush Foam® cushioned footbed
  • 100% vegan-friendly materials
  • Jute-wrapped midsole for stylish texture
  • Part of the BOBS® initiative to help save pets in need

Comfort Technologies

Hands Free Slip-ins®

Step in with ease and enjoy a secure fit. Skechers Hands Free Slip-ins® are built for ultimate convenience with no bending, no tugging—just comfort. The exclusive Heel Pillow™ supports your foot and keeps it in place for all-day wear.

Skechers Plush Foam®

The Plush Foam® footbed offers soft cushioning and lasting comfort underfoot, helping absorb impact and reduce pressure, step after step.

Key Features

  • Skechers Hands Free Slip-ins® back heel for easy on and off
  • Heel Pillow™ technology for secure heel placement and slip prevention
  • Skechers Plush Foam® cushioned comfort footbed
  • Crafted with 100% vegan materials for a more sustainable choice
  • Part of the BOBS® collection, supporting animal welfare efforts

Design Details

  • Soft linen upper with gore instep strap for flexibility and fit
  • Jute midsole trim adds a natural, breezy style
  • Flexible traction outsole for grip on multiple surfaces

  • BOBS® from Skechers® logo detail on upper and footbed

Why Choose This Style

The BOBS® Sun Ray - Bright Take is more than just a sandal—it’s a statement in comfort, sustainability, and compassion. With a hands-free design, premium materials, and a mission to support animal welfare, this style lets you look good, feel good, and do good—one step at a time.
