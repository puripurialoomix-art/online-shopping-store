Skechers GO WALK® Arch Fit® 2.0 – Idyllic 2

Experience advanced support and next-level comfort in the Skechers GO WALK® Arch Fit® 2.0 – Idyllic 2. Designed to deliver all-day performance, this lace-up walking shoe has been awarded the Seal of Acceptance by the American Podiatric Medical Association (APMA) for promoting good foot health. It features a breathable engineered mesh upper, the patented Arch Fit® insole system, and innovative ULTRA GO® cushioning technology with high-rebound Comfort Pillar Technology® for excellent support.

Key Features:

  • ✔ APMA Seal of Acceptance – recognized for promoting foot health
  • ✔ Patented Skechers Arch Fit® insole system with podiatrist-certified arch support
  • ✔ Podiatrist-designed shape based on 20 years of research and 120,000 unweighted foot scans
  • ✔ Removable insole helps mold to your foot, reducing shock and evenly dispersing weight
  • ✔ Lightweight and responsive ULTRA GO® cushioning for soft, energizing steps
  • ✔ Comfort Pillar Technology® for added structure and support with every step

Design Details:

  • ✓ Breathable, single-layer engineered mesh upper
  • ✓ Lace-up walking design for a secure, athletic fit
  • ✓ Dual-density traction outsole for enhanced grip and stability
  • ✓ 4.445 cm heel height for slight elevation and comfort
  • ✓ Machine washable for easy care
  • ✓ Subtle Skechers® and Arch Fit® logo accents

From daily walks to long hours on your feet, the Skechers GO WALK® Arch Fit® 2.0 – Idyllic 2 is built to support you every step of the way. Stylish, smart, and supportive — it’s everything your feet deserve.
