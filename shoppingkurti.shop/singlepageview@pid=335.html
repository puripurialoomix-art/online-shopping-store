Green Soul Vienna High Back Ergonomic Office Chair (Black)

Highlights

  • Ergonomic Comfort: Contoured, ultra-plush padding and integrated lumbar cushion for all-day back support.
  • Premium Material: Breathable bonded Leatherette upholstery with a sophisticated diamond pattern stitch.
  • Adjustable Features: Adjustable height, padded armrests, and a heavy-duty tilt mechanism (up to 135 degrees).
  • Durable & Stable: Wooden internal frame with a five-star reinforced resin base and 360-degree swivel.
  • Weight Capacity: Supports up to 110 kgs.
  • Recommended For: Office work, computer use, and all-day desk work.
Warranty: 5 Years Warranty
Policy: 15 Days Return and Refund Policy

Product Description

PREMIUM LOOK & FEEL: This chair provides superior luxury and comfort even during long, intense work hours. The premium thread-stitched diamond pattern provides a sophisticated detail that adds to the comfort.

ERGONOMIC SUPPORT: The chair features contoured, segmented ultra-plush padding, covered in breathable bonded leather for a luxurious feel. It provides all-day back support with an integrated lumbar cushion, perfect for preventing back and neck pain during long hours of work.

ADJUSTABLE FEATURES: This executive chair features an integrated headrest and padded arms for all-day comfort. The heavy-duty tilt mechanism allows the chair and seat to tilt back together up to 135 degrees or be locked at a 90-degree position for focused work. It also features a 360-degree swivel for easy movement.

Assembly Information

A toolkit and detailed instructions manual are included with the product for DIY assembly. A chargeable carpenter assembly service is also available; please contact customer support for further assistance.
