Skechers CORDOVA CLASSIC

Discover a perfect balance of breathable design, certified arch support, and eco-conscious comfort with the Skechers Arch Fit® – Rich Vision. Designed with your daily wellness in mind, this vegan-friendly sneaker combines cutting-edge footwear technology with classic style and flexibility for all-day wear.

Whether you're walking, working, or simply staying on your feet for long hours, the Arch Fit® - Rich Vision delivers dependable comfort and modern performance in every step. From its engineered knit upper to its removable insole and machine washable design, this shoe redefines support-driven footwear for today's lifestyle.

Product Highlights

  • Engineered knit upper with lace-up front
  • Removable Arch Fit® insole with podiatrist-certified arch support
  • Crafted with 100% vegan materials
  • Air-Cooled comfort for breathability
  • Machine washable for easy care

Comfort Technologies

Arch Fit® System

Developed with 20 years of data and 120,000 unweighted foot scans, the patented Skechers Arch Fit® insole system provides exceptional arch support. Designed and certified by podiatrists, it contours to your foot, reduces shock, and promotes even weight distribution to help reduce fatigue and discomfort.

Air-Cooled Design

Stay fresh and comfortable all day with the built-in Skechers Air-Cooled comfort feature. Breathable materials and moisture-wicking design help keep your feet cool and dry.

Key Features

  • Patented Skechers Arch Fit® insole system with podiatrist-certified arch support
  • Removable insole molds to your foot to reduce shock and disperse weight evenly
  • Engineered knit upper allows airflow and flexibility
  • Crafted using 100% vegan materials—no animal-derived components
  • Machine washable for convenient cleaning and long-term freshness

Design Details

  • Flexible traction outsole for a stable walking experience
  • Secure lace-up front for a snug fit
  • Heel height: 3.81 cm for added lift and comfort
  • Subtle Skechers® and Arch Fit® logo details

Why Choose Arch Fit® – Rich Vision?

If you’re looking for a shoe that blends podiatrist-approved support with a clean, breathable design and planet-friendly materials, the Skechers Arch Fit® - Rich Vision is your ideal companion. Lightweight, washable, and designed for comfort—this style supports your movement with purpose and performance.
