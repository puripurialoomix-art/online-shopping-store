Product Details

Modern Stretch Fit Sofa Slipcover for 2 Seater Sofa (Tan)

Highlights

  • Super Fit Material: Highly stretchable soft polyester spandex fabric (92% Polyester, 8% Spandex).
  • Wrinkle-Free Look: Covers your sofa tightly for a perfect, smooth effect.
  • All-Round Protection: Provides 360-degree coverage, protecting furniture from stains, pet hair, dirt, and wear.
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for most types of sofas, including L-shaped, armless, and futons.
  • Easy Care: Machine washable for simple maintenance.
Warranty: 5 Years Warranty
Policy: 15 Days Return and Refund Policy

Product Description

STRETCH SUPER FIT MATERIAL: Give your couch a perfect, wrinkle-free look with this highly stretchable soft polyester spandex fabric. The slipcover is designed to cover your sofa in a tightly knit way. Non-slip foam articles are provided to be put into the seat cushion creases to make the cover stay in place, providing a clean look with a soft and comfortable feel. Naturally dyed with no color shading or fading.

MULTIPURPOSE ALL-ROUND PROTECTION: This elastic universal couch slipcover provides total protection for your sofas, keeping your furniture away from stains, pet hair, dirt, claws, wear and tear, and other damages. It's great for families with kids, cats, and dogs or for anyone seeking an economical solution for furniture protection.

FRESHEN YOUR HOME DECOR: With a great variety of exquisite unique designs and colors, these slipcovers add elegance to your home decor, instantly brightening up your old furniture. The universal design suits normal 1/2/3/4 cushion couches, L-shaped sofas, armless sofas, futons, and sofa beds.

Important Sizing & Fit Note

  • These covers do not fit on recliner style sofas.
  • If your sofa is larger than the standard size (wider, larger back rests, attached cushions) or has high armrests, please purchase one size up. Extra fabric can always be tucked in.
  • For sectional sofas, ensure there are gaps and you may need to order more than 1 piece.
  • Actual product color may vary from the images shown due to different screen settings and lighting conditions.

Care Instructions

Machine wash on gentle cycle (preferably using a washing mesh bag) or bucket wash. Do not tumble dry. Do not bleach.
