Product Details

Contemporary Indian Geometric Print Co-ord Set

Step out in style with this Ecru Printed Co-ord Set, a perfect blend of comfort and contemporary design. This chic ensemble features a sleeveless A-line kurta and matching straight pants, both adorned with a striking geometric print in an evergreen black and white palette. Minimal Kantha stitch detailing adds a subtle, artistic touch, celebrating traditional craftsmanship.

Key Details

  • Colour: Ecru (White) with black detailing
  • Fabric: A comfortable and breathable woven blend of 88% Cotton and 12% Flax.
  • Pattern: Eye-catching Geometric Print
  • Ornamentation: Exquisite and subtle Kantha stitch work.
  • Fit: Regular Fit for all-day comfort.
  • Occasion: Perfect for casual outings and daily wear.
  • Care: Easy to maintain with Machine Wash.

Kurta Details

  • Neck: Flattering V-Neck
  • Sleeves: Sleeveless
  • Shape: A-Line Silhouette
  • Length: Elegant Calf Length
  • Slits: Side Slits for ease of movement
  • Hemline: Clean, Straight Hem
