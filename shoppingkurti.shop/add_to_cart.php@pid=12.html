Product Details

Skechers Aero Burst™ with Aero Comfort Technology™

Experience long-distance comfort and confidence with the Skechers Aero Burst™. Designed specifically for daily runners, this premium performance running shoe integrates Aero Comfort Technology™ for advanced support, cushioning, and responsiveness. Certified by the American Podiatric Medical Association (APMA), it promotes healthy foot motion while delivering high energy return with every step.

Product Highlights

  • APMA Seal of Acceptance
  • Hyper Burst Pro Cushioning
  • Carbon-Infused Plate Technology
  • Hyper ARC® Midsole Design
  • Goodyear® Rubber Performance Outsole
  • Machine Washable Construction
  • 100% Vegan Materials
  • Advanced Hyper Burst ICE™ Cushioning

Comfort Technologies

Arch Fit® Insole System

The patented Arch Fit® system includes a removable insole developed with 20 years of data and 120,000 foot scans. It molds to your foot for shock reduction and increased weight dispersion, providing a more comfortable walking and running experience.

Hyper Burst® Cushioning

Hyper Burst® is an ultra-lightweight and highly responsive cushioning system, offering soft landings and energy-packed push-offs. The Hyper Burst ICE™ version integrates dual-density foam for improved balance between softness and support.

Key Features

  • Certified by the American Podiatric Medical Association (APMA)
  • Dual-density HYPER BURST ICE™ midsole for softness and stability
  • Carbon-infused H-plate to enhance propulsion and energy return
  • Arch Fit® insole system with podiatrist-approved support
  • Removable insole for customized comfort and shock absorption
  • Goodyear® Performance Outsole for enhanced traction and durability

Design Details

  • Breathable, lace-up engineered mesh upper for airflow and secure fit
  • Highly cushioned trainer ideal for everyday running
  • HYPER ARC® adapts to your stride for smooth transitions
  • Stack Height: 42mm heel / 36mm forefoot
  • Heel-to-Toe Drop: 6mm
  • Weight: Approx. 10.9 oz / 309g (Men’s US Size 9)
  • Machine washable for easy care
  • Crafted entirely from vegan-friendly materials
  • Skechers Performance™ logo detailing
